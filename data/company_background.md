# Company Background

## Overview

**TechNova Inc.** is a Series B AI infrastructure startup founded in 2022, focused on providing enterprise-grade AI model training and inference solutions. We help Fortune 500 companies deploy machine learning workloads at scale without building in-house GPU clusters.

## Mission

Democratize enterprise AI by making GPU infrastructure as accessible and scalable as cloud compute.

## Product

### Core Offering: NovaScale Platform

The NovaScale platform is a managed AI infrastructure service that provides:

- **Auto-scaling GPU clusters** on major cloud providers (AWS, GCP, Azure)
- **Multi-tenant isolation** for enterprise security requirements
- **Model optimization** tools for 2-5x cost reduction
- **Monitoring and observability** for ML workflows
- **Compliance certifications**: SOC2, HIPAA, FedRAMP ready

### Pricing Model

- **Pay-per-use**: $0.50-2.00 per GPU hour based on GPU type
- **Enterprise contracts**: Monthly commitments with volume discounts
- **No long-term contracts**: Flexibility for workloads with variable demand
